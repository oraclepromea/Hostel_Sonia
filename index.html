About Us

Beautiful and comfortable hostel located in the center of Copacabana, we have single and double rooms for a perfect stay.

Hostal Sonia offers accommodation in Copacabana with free WiFi access and daily breakfast included.

Located 200 meters from the main square and 100 meters from the Cathedral of Our Lady of Copacabana. The Horcas del Inca are 200 meters from Hostal Sonia, while the Copacabana Market is 400 meters from the property.

Comfortable Rooms

Rooms equipped with cable TV, mountain or garden views, and private bathroom.

Restaurant

On-site restaurant and breakfast included to start your day right.

Free Parking

Free private parking and transportation services available.
